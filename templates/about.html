Home page:

This where you started. This is the main page that represents other peoples experiences. On top, you can find useful links that are always available to you, no matter where you find your self on the page. If you are on a smaller screen, you wil notice three horizontal bars. Tap them and you will be able to see the links.

Search

If you want to find any specific term, this is the place to go. Write on the line what you are looking for, and tap/click on Search. Your search query will give you results for all entries in each experience except recommendation and user. Those can be selected by a dropdown filter below.

Sort by

Here you can sort the experiences by user, recommendation and country. Just click/tap on the dropdown menu and select your filter.

Floating icon

Click/tap on the green icon in lower right corner. If you are not logged in, it will offer you to login or register. Once you log in, this icon will be your best friend on this page. When you are logged in, it expands into add new experience, your profile page and log out.

Your profile page

Once you log in, your profile page will open. Form there, you will be able to add experiences, edit your experiences and delete them

Create experience

To add experience, click/tap here and a new window will open.

From there follow these steps:

  1. Upload an image by clicking/tapping on "Browse"
  2. From the pop up window navigate to the image you want to upload.
  3. You can now see a preview of the image and if you are not satisfied with it, repeat step 1
  4. Once you are happy with your selection, click/tap on Next. Please have in mind, once you select the image, you won't be able to go back untill you finish the experience creation. If you changed your mind, click on cancel
  5. Fill in the fields and pay attention to required fields. They will have red underline if you miss them and you won't be able to proceed.
  6. When you fill in the necessary fields, click on Save
  7. Your experience is now created and others can see it too.

After you have added your experience, it will lead you to your profile page where you can see all of your experiences. You can edit or delete them.

Edit details

If you want to edit details of your experience, find the one you want, click on Edit and follow the steps for creating the experience.

Delete experience

To delete your experience, go to your page (if you are not already there), and click/tap on delete. Be carefull, because once you delete it, there is no way to restore it.
